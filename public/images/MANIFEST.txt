Nordora Vital — public/images layout
=====================================

This tree mirrors material from the project References folder (see /Website/References).

references/     Curated web assets (hero, pillars, modular, sectors, offers, lifestyle photos).
brand/          Nordora spiral logo (PNG) and legacy placeholders.
sanzanet/       Traceability copy of selected sanza reference files (e.g. hero source PNG).

references/lifestyle/  Warm lifestyle JPGs from References/sanzanet/images/happy-aging-main

To refresh from References after adding files there:
  bash web/scripts/sync-reference-images.sh

Replace placeholders under references/ with final photography when available; keep filenames stable or update src/lib/public-images.ts.
