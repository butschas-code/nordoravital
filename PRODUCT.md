# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Nordora Vital serves professional practice owners, therapists, clinic operators, physiotherapists, dentists, sports and recovery facilities, spa and wellness teams, and premium service businesses that care about how a visit feels from arrival to departure.

Visitors are usually evaluating whether sanza can fit into an existing professional workflow without disrupting clinical work, staff routines, compliance requirements, or the trust they have built with patients and clients. They need to understand the benefit for both sides: the professional gains a calmer, more premium, more differentiated visit experience; the patient or client receives a smoother arrival, more comfort, and a softer transition back into the day.

## Product Purpose

The website exists to turn professional curiosity into direct conversations with Nordora Vital. Its primary conversion goal is a clinic demo or information request, not instant checkout. The site should help visitors feel that sanza is credible, easy to integrate, premium, and operationally realistic.

sanza is positioned as a professional in-room wellbeing system that complements care and service experiences. It uses PEMF, structured frequency programs, and targeted applicators to support comfort, calm, relaxation, and a more complete visit. It must not be presented as a medical treatment, rehabilitation protocol, cure, diagnostic tool, or replacement for clinical work.

Success means more qualified professionals contacting Nordora Vital for a demo, a pilot discussion, or more information, while leaving with a clear understanding that sanza supports the surrounding experience of care.

## Brand Personality

Calm, professional, tactile.

Nordora Vital should feel grounded and trustworthy, with the warmth of a high-end treatment room and the clarity of a serious professional partner. The voice should speak directly to practitioners and owners, respecting their expertise rather than overselling to them.

The emotional center is reassurance: you already do meaningful work; sanza helps the visit around that work feel calmer, more complete, and more valuable.

## Anti-references

The site must not feel like a generic AI-generated wellness landing page. Avoid purple-blue gradients, neon health-tech styling, glassmorphism as decoration, nested cards, generic icon grids, hero stat templates, fake medical claims, miracle-treatment language, and vague startup copy such as "elevate", "unleash", "next-gen", or "seamless".

It should not look like a hospital dashboard, crypto product, SaaS analytics page, beauty influencer brand, or commodity e-commerce shop. Pricing and hardware details support the conversation, but the core story is professional fit and visit quality.

Do not imply clinical outcomes, disease treatment, rehabilitation results, pain relief guarantees, or regulatory approval beyond what is explicitly documented. The site must remain compliant and careful.

## Design Principles

1. Speak to the professional directly.
   Address the therapist, clinic owner, or practice team as the expert. sanza is the supporting layer around their work, not the hero replacing it.

2. Make the benefit bilateral.
   Every important page should clarify how the practitioner or practice benefits and how the patient or client benefits.

3. Show workflow fit before feature detail.
   The most persuasive question is "where does this fit in my day?" Lead with moments in the visit, staff use, room flow, and demo reassurance before technical abstraction.

4. Premium without hype.
   Use calm confidence, precise language, and strong imagery. Avoid inflated claims, urgency tricks, miracle language, and decorative excess.

5. Compliance is part of the brand.
   Keep claims clear, limited, and careful. Separate wellbeing support from clinical treatment. Make this distinction feel reassuring, not defensive.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

The site should target WCAG AA contrast and preserve visible focus states for all keyboard navigation. Touch targets should remain at least 44px on mobile. Motion should respect `prefers-reduced-motion`, with animations used to clarify hierarchy and state rather than distract.

Language support is core to the product experience. English, German, and Latvian pages should preserve the same intent, not merely literal translations. Localized copy must keep compliance boundaries intact.
